Company Background

LuftCrete Philippines is a company that operates as a subsidiary of a well- established firm that specializes in the production and distribution of ready-mixed concrete in Cebu. The name "LuftCrete" is a combination of the German word "Luft," which means "air," and "Crete," which is the shortened word for concrete. This reflects the unique properties of the pricing, which is a lightweight, highly insulating building material that provides exceptional strength and durability. The sister company, widely known as Asia Concrete Solutions, is a leading player in the industry with a proven track record of delivering high-quality products and services to its customers.

The company recognized the need for a lightweight, durable, and eco-friendly building material to meet the growing demand for sustainable construction solutions. As a result, LuftCrete was founded with LuftPanel and LuftBlocks.

LuftPanel and LuftBlocks are types of precast foam concrete that offer superior insulation, fire resistance, and acoustic performance compared to traditional building materials. These are made from a mixture of cement, fly ash, water, and an expanding agent. With the sister company's extensive experience in the ready-mixed concrete industry, LuftCrete has been able to leverage its expertise in production, logistics, and quality control to ensure consistent and reliable pricing supply to its customers.

Lighter weight

We are significantly lighter than traditional building materials, which reduces the overall weight of the building and allows for larger structures to be constructed without additional support.

Energy efficiency

We got an excellent thermal insulation property, reducing the amount of energy needed to heat and cool buildings. This can lead to significant cost savings in energy bills over time.

Fire resistance

LuftCrete has a high resistance to fire, making it a safer option for buildings in high-risk areas or those that require a higher level of fire protection.

Sound insulation

LuftCrete has excellent sound insulation properties, reducing noise pollution inside and outside of buildings.

Durability

LuftCrete is highly durable and resistant to impact, weather, and other types of wear and tear, resulting in a longer lifespan for buildings constructed with this material.

Sustainability

LuftCrete is made from natural materials and has a lower environmental impact than traditional building materials. It is also recyclable and can be reused in other building projects.

Easy installation

LuftCrete can be easily cut, shaped, and installed, making it a faster and more efficient option for construction projects.
